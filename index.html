Foundation of LVPA

November 9, 2006: A group of students of the Latin Circle of Prague reads the fairytale Parva Rubra Cuculla (Little Red Riding Hood), translated into Latin by Karel Treska. One of the students recognizes herself as the main heroine of the famous story. She immediately suggests performing the fairytale at the annual Christmas party of the Department of Classics at the Charles University in Prague.

December 20, 2006: Premiere of the performance Parva Rubra Cuculla in the presence of a numerous audience. The old story however is unthinkable without the presence of a wolf, resp. a she-wolf. Therefore not only has Parva Rubra Cuculla been introduced to the audience, but also the she-wolf (LVPA1,2).
